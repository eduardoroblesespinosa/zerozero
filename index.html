A new drone for a new decade
Brand new V-shaped dual-rotor propulsion system combined with proprietary tilt rotor technology, and nonlinear dynamic modeling and control algorithms enable 50min extra long flight time.

50-minute flight time

V-shaped dual-rotor propulsion system is more efficient than quadcopters of the same size under the same payload.

Lower noise profile

Slower rotation of the propellers and patented propeller design create lift with higher efficiency and less noise.

Tilt rotor technology

Sophisticated industrial design, nonlinear dynamic modeling and control algorithms empower stable flight with high agility.

3-axis mechanical gimbal

Stable videos with yaw control range of ±80 degrees.

4K Crystal Clear Video
1/2.3 inch 12 MP CMOS sensor
Autofollow
Upgraded visual tracking algorithms allow V-Coptr Falcon to follow the target accurately and stably.
Obstacle Avoidance
V-Coptr Falcon is equipped with advanced VIO (Visual Inertial Odometry) and a front facing stereo camera. It can map its surroundings, detect obstacles, and avoid collisions in realtime.
Trackshots
Pre-programmed flight paths help you achieve cinematic effects.
7 Kilometer Transmission Distance
With 50 minutes of flight time and 7 kilometer transmission distance, fly as high and far as you’d like.
